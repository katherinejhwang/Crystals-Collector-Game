How to Play

* Match "Your Score" to the "Crystal Score"
* Each of the four crystals has a different value. Click on a crystal to add a specific amount of points to the player's total score.
* You must match the score exactly to win.
* If you go pass the "Crystal Score", then you lose.
* The game will restart whenever you win or lose. When the game restarts, the crystals will all have new numbers.
